Chicken Lollipop Recipe

Chicken lollipop is a popular Indo-Chinese appetizer where a frenched chicken drumette is marinated and then batter fried or baked until crisp.

Ingredients:

  • 8-10 chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon of red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Clean and wash the chicken wings thoroughly.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, cornstarch, and salt to make a marinade.
  3. Add the chicken wings to the marinade and coat them evenly. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan or fryer to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Carefully drop the marinated chicken wings into the hot oil and fry until they are golden brown and crispy, about 8-10 minutes.
  6. Remove the chicken lollipops from the oil and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  7. Your delicious chicken lollipops are ready to be served. Enjoy!
